What is the death toll and the aftermath of the flooding in Libya caused by storm Daniel?

At 2023-sep-12, as many as 20,000 people are believed to have died due to catastrophic flooding in Libya following torrential rains brought by storm Daniel. Rescue workers in the city of Derna are struggling to cope with the large number of bodies under the rubble and in the water, and there are fears of an outbreak of an epidemic.
