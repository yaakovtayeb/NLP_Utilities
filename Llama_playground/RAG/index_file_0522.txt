What happened during the early morning raid on al-Shifa hospital in Gaza and what was the Israeli army's justification for the operation?

At 2023-nov-15, israeli soldiers entered al-Shifa hospital during an early morning raid and conducted a "precise and targeted operation against Hamas in a specified area". The Israeli military official claimed that weapons and other terror infrastructure had been found during the operation "in one specific area". The hospital staff and patients were reportedly in a different section of the site and there was no fighting inside the hospital complex. The raid has drawn fierce condemnation from the head of the World Health Organization, who called it "totally unacceptable".
