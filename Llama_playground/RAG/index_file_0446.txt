What is Israel's response to the condemnation of sexual violence by Hamas during the attack on Israel?

At 2023-dec-06, israel criticized the head of the United Nations children’s agency, Catherine Russell, for issuing a statement condemning the acts of sexual violence committed against women during Hamas’s deadly assault on Israel on 7 October. Israel believed that the statement was insufficient and was only issued as a result of international pressure. The Israeli Prime Minister, Benjamin Netanyahu, also criticized aid and rights agencies over the issue of sexual violence. President Joe Biden called on governments and international organizations to "forcefully condemn the sexual violence of Hamas terrorists without equivocation."
