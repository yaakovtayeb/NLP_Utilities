What is the warning that Joe Biden gave to Benjamin Netanyahu during his recent visit to Israel?

At 2023-oct-20, joe Biden warned Benjamin Netanyahu not to be consumed by rage and to learn from the mistakes made by the US after the 9/11 attacks, cautioning against the risk of Israel making decisions it may come to regret in its fury at Hamas. Biden reminded Netanyahu that the US invaded Afghanistan and Iraq, both with devastating consequences, after the 9/11 attacks.
