Why was Mahsa Yazdani sentenced to 13 years in prison in Iran?

At 2023-nov-02, mahsa Yazdani was sentenced to 13 years in prison in Iran after she demanded justice for her son Mohammad Javad Zahedi, who was killed at an anti-regime protest in September 2022. She was convicted on charges of blasphemy, incitement, insulting the supreme leader, and spreading anti-regime propaganda.
