What does Ami Ayalon suggest Israel should do to achieve security, and what is his opinion on the current operation in Gaza?

At 2024-jan-14, ami Ayalon suggests that Israel should release Marwan Barghouti, jailed leader of the second intifada, to direct negotiations to create a Palestinian state. He also believes that destroying Hamas is not a realistic military goal and the current operation in Gaza risks entrenching support for the group.
