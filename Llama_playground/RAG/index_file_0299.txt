What are the new sanctions that the US and UK will impose on Houthi leaders, and when will they be announced?

At 2024-jan-25, the US and UK will impose new sanctions on leaders of the Iran-aligned Houthi group, which will include at least four senior figures being subject to asset freezes and travel bans. Senior ministers in the Houthi administration in Yemen would also be sanctioned, with an announcement expected as early as Thursday.
