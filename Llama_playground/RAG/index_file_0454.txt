What happened during the meeting between Benjamin Netanyahu and the recently released hostages and relatives of Israelis still held by Hamas in Gaza?

At 2023-dec-05, the meeting was angry and chaotic, with some representatives of the hostage families walking out. Relatives of those still in captivity and recently returned hostages spoke, with some describing mental and physical abuse at the hands of their captors. Netanyahu read from pre-prepared remarks and some present reportedly called on him to resign.
