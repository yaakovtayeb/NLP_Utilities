What was the reason behind the US strikes in Iraq and what were the targets hit?

At 2024-jan-22, the US strikes in Iraq were in retaliation for missile and drone attacks on American troops in Iraq and Syria over the past several days. The strikes hit militia facilities in Jurf al-Sakhar, which is south of Baghdad, al-Qaim and another unnamed site in western Iraq, two US officials told AP. The strikes were carried out against the Iranian-backed Kataib Hezbollah militia group and other Iran-affiliated groups in Iraq.
