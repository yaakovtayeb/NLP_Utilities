What is the role of artificial intelligence in Israel's bombing campaign in Gaza?

At 2023-dec-01, israel Defense Forces (IDF) is using an AI target-creation platform called "the Gospel" to produce targets at a fast pace, which has significantly accelerated a lethal production line of targets that officials have compared to a "factory". The Gospel has allowed the IDF to run a "mass assassination factory" in which the "emphasis is on quantity and not on quality".
