What is the lawsuit being filed against Germany regarding arms sales to Israel, and who are the lead litigants?

At 2024-apr-12, the lawsuit being filed against Germany asks judges to urgently direct the government to revoke all arms licenses to Israel issued since October 7, 2023, and is being issued by four human rights groups on behalf of five named Palestinians who say they are in fear of their lives in Gaza, and are suffering a form of collective punishment by Israel. One of the lead litigants is the European Center for Constitutional and Human Rights (ECCHR).
