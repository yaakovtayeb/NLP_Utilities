What is the current status of the conflict between Israel and Hamas in Gaza and what is the US stance on a ceasefire?

At 2023-nov-05, the conflict has entered its fifth week and there is growing global anger over the rising death toll among Palestinian civilians in the Gaza Strip. The US is pushing for a more temporary pause in fighting amid calls for a ceasefire, but the top US diplomat dismissed the idea, saying such a halt would only benefit Hamas.
