What are the economic risks posed by the escalating tensions in the Middle East and the disruption to global trade?

At 2024-jan-14, the escalating tensions in the Middle East and the disruption to global trade pose economic risks such as rising oil prices, lengthy delays to international shipping, empty shelves, higher prices, and component shortages. The disruption to global trade through key shipping lanes could also increase global energy prices, particularly for the UK, which has increased its reliance on fossil fuel imports from the Middle East.
