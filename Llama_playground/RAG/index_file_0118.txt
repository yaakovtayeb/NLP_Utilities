What did Bernie Sanders say in his message to Benjamin Netanyahu regarding the situation in Gaza?

At 2024-apr-02, bernie Sanders asked Benjamin Netanyahu to "Stop murdering innocent people." in an interview with MSNBC on Tuesday, a day after seven aid workers were killed by an Israeli strike in Gaza. Sanders also mentioned that "Two-thirds of the people who have been killed, over 32,000 people have been killed [in Gaza], are women and children. This is inexcusable."
