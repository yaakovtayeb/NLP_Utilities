What do we know so far about the Hamas and Israel conflict?

At 2023-oct-07, hamas militants entered Israeli territory and fired thousands of rockets towards Israel, resulting in at least 150 Israeli deaths and more than 1,100 Israelis being wounded. In retaliation, at least 198 people have been killed and 1,610 wounded in Gaza by Israeli retaliation after the Hamas attack. The conflict has led to reports of Israeli hostages in Gaza, and the UN security council is due to meet to discuss the situation.
