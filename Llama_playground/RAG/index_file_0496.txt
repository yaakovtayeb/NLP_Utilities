What is the situation like for medical staff in Southern Gaza's European hospital?

At 2023-nov-24, medical staff in Southern Gaza's European hospital are overwhelmed and facing an acute lack of medicine, food rations, and communication. They are making triage decisions on who among the huge influx of patients arriving from the north of Gaza should live or die. They are struggling to cope with the number of casualties, along with thousands of displaced people seeking shelter and safety in medical facilities.
