What prompted Israel to signal its intent to launch a ground assault in southern Gaza? 

At 2023-dec-01, israel attacked the Strip on Friday after the end of a seven-day truce, leaving at least 178 Palestinians dead. The country's military dropped leaflets in parts of Khan Younis, where Israel believes Hamas's leadership is based, warning citizens to evacuate farther south to Rafah, prompting humanitarian groups to say that Palestinians risked being forced completely out of the territory.
