What is the book Palestine by Joe Sacco about and why has it been rushed back into print? 

At 2023-dec-10, palestine is a nonfiction graphic novel about Gaza that was created by Joe Sacco, a Maltese American journalist and cartoonist from Portland, Oregon, as a record of his own journeys around Gaza in 1991. It has won a clutch of awards and been included on university courses as a primer for the whole conflict. It has been rushed back into print after surging demand since the fresh outbreak of the conflict two months ago.
