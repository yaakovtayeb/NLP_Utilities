How many Palestinians are being held in Israeli jails and what are the conditions like?

At 2023-dec-21, more than 8,000 Palestinians are being held in Israeli jails, with 80% of detainees being held in administrative detention, and the conditions have been described as harsh and inhumane by human rights groups. Many are teenage boys and young men accused of throwing stones and firebombs or associating with militants. Some have been held for months or even years without charge.
