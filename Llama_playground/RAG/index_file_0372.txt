Who claimed responsibility for the attack in southern Iran and what was the government's response?

At 2024-jan-04, islamic State claimed responsibility for the attack in southern Iran that killed at least 84 people, and the Iranian government vowed to strengthen security along its borders with Afghanistan and Pakistan. The Revolutionary Guards promised a harsh response, describing the attack as a “blind and spiteful act to induce insecurity in the country and take revenge on the love and devotion of the great nation of Iran”.
