What is the case being heard at the International Court of Justice in The Hague?

At 2024-jan-11, the International Court of Justice is hearing arguments alleging that Israel is committing genocide in Gaza. South Africa, which has brought the case, is asking the UN court to act urgently “to protect against further, severe and irreparable harm to the rights of the Palestinian people under the genocide convention, which continues to be violated with impunity”.
