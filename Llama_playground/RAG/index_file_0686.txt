What happened to Tamar, and why is her family worried about her?

At 2023-oct-16, tamar Gutman, the daughter of the author, went missing after attending a music festival near Kibbutz Re’im, which became a Hamas killing field during the atrocious events that unfolded in southern Israel on 7 October. Her best friend and another friend from their group were found dead at the site. Her family believes she is being held hostage by Hamas in Gaza. Tamar suffers from Crohn’s disease, a debilitating inflammatory bowel illness that has made her dependent on medication, a careful diet and recurring hospital visits. She is in urgent need of medical care.
