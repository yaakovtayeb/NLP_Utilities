What is the National Commission for the Missing and Forcibly Disappeared in Lebanon, and what is its purpose?

At 2024-feb-27, the National Commission for the Missing and Forcibly Disappeared was established in Lebanon in 2018 to locate and exhume mass graves and work to trace individuals who went missing during the civil war. Its purpose is to investigate the fate of people who disappeared and to acquire information that could help unravel the fate of thousands of missing people.
