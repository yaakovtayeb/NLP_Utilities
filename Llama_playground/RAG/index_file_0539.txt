What is the current situation in Gaza's hospitals according to Médecins Sans Frontières? 

At 2023-nov-11, according to Médecins Sans Frontières, patients and medical staff in Gaza are “trapped in hospitals under fire” and hospitals in Gaza have been under relentless bombardment. Al-Shifa hospital complex, the biggest health facility where MSF staff are still working, has been hit several times, including the maternity and outpatient departments, resulting in multiple deaths and injuries.
