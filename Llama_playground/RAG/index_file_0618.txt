What happened to the family of the British-Israeli lawyer, Ahal Besorai, who were taken hostage by Hamas?

At 2023-oct-27, ahal Besorai's sister, Yonat Or, her husband, Dror, and two of their three children were taken hostage by Hamas militants. Yonat's body was identified among those murdered at the kibbutz, while the rest of the family remains missing.
