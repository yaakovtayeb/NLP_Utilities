What is happening to American citizens trapped in Gaza, and what legal actions are being taken to help them?

At 2023-nov-01, american citizens are trapped in the Gaza Strip due to heavy bombardment by Israel since Hamas's attacks on 7 October. Nearly a dozen lawsuits have been filed or are set to be filed against the US state department by Palestinian-Americans who received disparate treatment in repatriation efforts when compared with efforts to evacuate US citizens in Israel.
