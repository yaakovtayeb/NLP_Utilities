What are some recommended books to understand the Israel-Palestine conflict?

At 2024-feb-29, out of It by Selma Dabbagh, Rise and Kill First: The Secret History of Israel’s Targeted Assassinations by Ronen Bergman, Return to Haifa by Ghassan Kanafani, Apples from the Desert by Savyon Liebrecht, and The Hundred Years’ War on Palestine by Rashid Khalidi are some of the recommended books to understand the Israel-Palestine conflict.
