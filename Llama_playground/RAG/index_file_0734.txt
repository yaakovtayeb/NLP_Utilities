What is the current situation in Gaza as the sole power plant shuts down?

At 2023-oct-11, the sole power plant in Gaza has run out of fuel amid a tightening siege, causing hospitals and homes to lose power. This has led to a catastrophic situation with people seeking shelter in hospitals, and many trapped under the rubble of their destroyed homes. The Israeli government has decided to completely cut off water and electricity supplies, and the phone network has been badly damaged.
