What was Manahel al-Otaibi sentenced to and why was she arrested in Saudi Arabia? 

At 2024-may-01, manahel al-Otaibi, a women's rights activist, was sentenced to 11 years in prison by an anti-terrorism court in Saudi Arabia for "terrorist offenses" related to her use of social media to promote women's rights and her choice of clothing. She was arrested in November 2022 and accused of using a hashtag to call for an end to male guardianship rules.
