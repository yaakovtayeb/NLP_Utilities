What is the situation in southern Gaza as Israeli forces move closer to crowded areas?
The situation in southern Gaza is deeply concerning as Israeli forces move closer to areas crowded with more than a million people seeking refuge from the destruction across the rest of the territory. The prospect of major operations taking place in territory with such a dense and vulnerable population is “deeply concerning”, say aid officials, who fear Gaza’s largest remaining hospital may have to be closed or evacuated.
