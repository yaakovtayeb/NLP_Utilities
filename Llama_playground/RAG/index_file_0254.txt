What is the current situation in Rafah and what is the Biden administration's stance on the Israeli military campaign against Hamas?

At 2024-feb-09, israel is moving closer to a full-scale ground offensive against the southern Gaza city of Rafah, where about 1.3 million displaced Palestinians are sheltering. Despite warnings from a senior aid official and the UN, Israel appeared determined to push ahead. The US President Joe Biden described Israel’s military response in Gaza as “over the top” and said he was seeking a “sustained pause” in fighting. The US specifically warned against attacking Rafah and said it would not support it.
