What did Elizabeth El-Nakla say about the conditions in Gaza and where is she trapped?

At 2023-oct-13, elizabeth El-Nakla, the mother-in-law of Scotland's first minister, asked "where is humanity" and pleaded for help as she described the conditions in Gaza under Israeli bombardment. She is trapped in Gaza with her husband since Israel's bombardment of Gaza in response to a deadly attack by Hamas at the weekend.
