What is domicide and how does it affect targeted communities? 

At 2024-jan-30, domicide is the systematic and deliberate killing of home, which causes deep suffering to targeted communities through the killing or displacement of people, the destruction of their memory, and the targeting of their sense of dignity and identity. Destroyers and occupiers use the mass destruction of the material culture of targeted communities to send a clear message to the victims that they have no place on this land.
