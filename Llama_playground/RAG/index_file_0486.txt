What happened to Nofuz Hammad, a 16-year-old Palestinian girl who was supposed to be released from Israeli prison under the ceasefire deal with Hamas?

At 2023-nov-27, nofuz Hammad was supposed to be released from Israeli prison under the ceasefire deal with Hamas, but her father could not find her at the prison. Later, the family's lawyers found out that Nofuz had been transferred back to Damon prison, and it was alleged that she was beaten and spent at least one night in the hospital before being sent back.
