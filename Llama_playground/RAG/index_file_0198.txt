What actions can the EU take to put pressure on Israel to end the war in Gaza?

At 2024-mar-04, the EU can use its leverage as Israel's largest trading partner to potentially suspend the EU-Israel trade agreement, impose sanctions and travel bans on violent West Bank settlers, individually sanction politicians who appear to have made exhortations to genocide or ethnic cleansing, and make it clear that it will unilaterally recognize Palestinian statehood based on 1967 borders.
