What was the main outcome of the ceasefire deal between Israel and Hamas?

At 2023-nov-24, the ceasefire deal between Israel and Hamas resulted in the release of 24 hostages, including 13 Israelis, 10 Thais, and a Philippine national, as well as the release of 39 Palestinian prisoners. It also marked the first break in seven weeks of war in Gaza and offered some relief for the 2.3 million Palestinians who have endured intensive Israeli bombardment, as well as for families in Israel fearful for the fate of their loved ones taken captive during the attack that triggered the conflict.
