What has been the impact of the renewed bombing campaign on the people of Gaza?

At 2023-dec-02, the renewed bombing campaign has caused fear and despair among the people of Gaza, with many feeling helpless and regretting their decision to move to the south. The psychological situation of children has been impacted, with many feeling threatened and distressed. The bombing has also caused displacement, overcrowding, and a shortage of medical supplies, fuel, and aid deliveries. Trauma and illness are becoming increasingly common, with many families struggling to find basic necessities like baby milk.
