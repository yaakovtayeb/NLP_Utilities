Who does Ed Davey suggest the UK should impose sanctions on and why?

At 2024-feb-29, ed Davey suggests that the UK should impose sanctions on two far-right Israeli ministers, Itamar Ben-Gvir and Bezalel Smotrich, who have pushed for more settlements on Palestinian land. This is vital to stop the fighting in Gaza spreading to the West Bank and to prevent the collapse of the prospect of a two-state solution.
