What happened in Tel Aviv's Hostages Square after news of the hostage release was confirmed?

At 2023-nov-24, several hundred people cheered and sang songs as they waited for the confirmation of the release of 13 women and children held by Hamas. The names of those being released began to filter out, and their ages ranged from two to 85. The released hostages were taken to Egypt and then to Israel for medical examinations and a face-to-face reunion with their relatives.
