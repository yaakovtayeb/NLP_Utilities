What is happening at the Indonesian hospital in northern Gaza and how many people are believed to have been killed?

At 2023-nov-20, israeli forces are closing in on the Indonesian hospital in northern Gaza, and 12 people are believed to have been killed in shelling. The hospital is the last one operating in northern Gaza, and 700 people, including medical staff, are crowded into it. Efforts are being made to coordinate a rescue of patients with the Red Cross, although there have been no commitments from Israel about allowing safe passage.
