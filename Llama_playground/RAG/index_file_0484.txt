What is the oil demand sustainability programme (ODSP) and what are its objectives?

At 2023-nov-27, the ODSP is a Saudi Arabian investment plan to create demand for its oil and gas in developing countries. Its objective is to accelerate the development of supersonic air travel, partner with a carmaker to mass produce a cheap combustion engine vehicle, promote power ships, and increase the use of fossil fuel-powered cars, buses, and planes in Africa and elsewhere, as rich countries increasingly switch to clean energy.
