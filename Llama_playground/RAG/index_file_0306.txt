What is the current situation in Khan Younis, Gaza, and why are thousands of people trapped in hospitals?

At 2024-jan-24, thousands of people in Khan Younis are trapped in hospitals due to Israel's assault on the Gaza Strip's second-largest city. The Israeli army has encircled the southern city of Khan Younis after two days of heavy fighting, making it difficult for civilians to flee. Fierce battles have reached the gates of Khan Younis's three main hospitals, making it hard for civilians to escape.
