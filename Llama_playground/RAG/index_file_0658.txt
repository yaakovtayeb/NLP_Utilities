What is the main message conveyed in the article about the Israel-Gaza crisis?

At 2023-oct-20, the article emphasizes that governments fail to represent their people as they struggle to make sense of the horror of the recent slaughter in Israel and Gaza. The author argues that humanity should guide us better than world leaders, and that violence only breeds more violence. The tactical response to remove the capacity of Hamas to conduct future atrocities against civilians is also discussed.
