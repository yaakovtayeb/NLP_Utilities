What is the international court of justice in The Hague ruling on regarding Israel and South Africa, and when will the verdict be delivered?

At 2024-jan-24, the international court of justice in The Hague is delivering its verdict on the request for provisional measures submitted by South Africa in the case against Israel regarding the violation of the rights of the Palestinian people under the genocide convention. The verdict will be delivered on Friday, 26 January 2024, at 1200 GMT.
