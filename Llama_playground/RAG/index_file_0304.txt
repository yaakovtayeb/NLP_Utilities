What happened to the US navy cargo ships off the coast of Yemen?

At 2024-jan-24, the US navy cargo ships belonging to Maersk came under attack from three anti-ship missiles near the Bab el-Mandeb strait. Two missiles were shot down, and a third landed in the sea. No damage was caused either to the Maersk Detroit or the Maersk Chesapeake and no injuries were reported. The US navy escorted both ships back to the Gulf of Aden.
