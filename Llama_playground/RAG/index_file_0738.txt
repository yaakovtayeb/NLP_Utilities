What is the current death toll and number of casualties in the conflict between Hamas and Israel?

At 2023-oct-10, the Palestinian casualty figures as of Tuesday are at least 770 killed and 4,000 wounded by Israeli airstrikes since Saturday, with an additional 18 people killed and 100 injured in the West Bank since Saturday. Israeli media reports that Hamas fighters killed about 900 people inside Israel during the weekend incursion.
