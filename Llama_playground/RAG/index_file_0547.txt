What is the story of Amjed Tantesh and his swimming school in Gaza?

At 2023-nov-09, amjed Tantesh is a swimming coach in Gaza who has taught thousands of children to swim in his pools on Gaza's beaches. He has survived previous wars but now dreads the news of his former students being killed or injured. He mourns the deaths of his students and friends on Facebook. He has coached in Gaza since 1999 and initially dreamed of producing an Olympic competitor but his concern for the safety of the territory's children was a key driver.
