What is the current situation in Gaza and how is it affecting the people living there?

At 2023-dec-30, israeli planes have been bombing refugee camps in Gaza, causing tens of thousands of Palestinians to flee their homes. The attacks have killed 165 people in 24 hours, including many women and children. The UN has warned that 40% of the population is at risk of famine and there are shortages of food, clean water, and medical supplies due to the Israeli blockade. Many Gazans have fled their homes and are living in overcrowded and unsafe conditions.
