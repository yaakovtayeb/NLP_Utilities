What is the current situation in the conflict between Israel and Hamas in the Middle East?

At 2023-oct-11, the conflict between Israel and Hamas in the Middle East has resulted in a high death toll, with more than 1,200 people killed. Hamas has been responsible for massacres in southern Israel, while Israel has bombed densely populated areas in Gaza, resulting in the deaths of many civilians. The international community is urging Israel to abide by humanitarian law and protect civilians, while also condemning the use of disinformation to promote agendas.
