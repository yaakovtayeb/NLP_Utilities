What was the impact of the end of the ceasefire on Gaza's population?

At 2023-dec-01, the end of the ceasefire had a devastating impact on Gaza's population, with over 100 people killed and hospitals struggling with a new influx of patients. The fresh waves of attacks hit a population physically weakened by weeks without access to adequate food, clean water or sanitation, traumatised by the damage of the war, and largely displaced from their homes.
