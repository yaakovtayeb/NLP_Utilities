What is the Rafah border crossing and why is it important?
The Rafah border crossing is the only potential exit for most Palestinians in the Gaza Strip and it represents the territory's sole remaining lifeline to the outside world. Egypt controls the crossing under a 2007 agreement with Israel, but supplies coming into Gaza through Rafah require Israeli approval.
