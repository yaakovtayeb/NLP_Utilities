What is Donald Trump's stance on the Israel-Gaza conflict and how does it differ from Joe Biden's? 

At 2024-apr-25, donald Trump blames Joe Biden for the crisis, claiming it "would not have happened" if he had been president. He has criticized Netanyahu and Israeli intelligence for failing to anticipate and stop the invasion, and he has urged Israel to "finish up your war." Trump has promised, if elected, to cut off all US aid to Palestinians and urged other nations to follow suit. He has not outlined how he believes peace might be achieved or what he envisions for the region after the conflict ends.
