What was discussed at the "Victory of Israel Conference" attended by Israeli ministers and parliamentarians?

At 2024-jan-29, the conference called for Israeli resettlement of the Gaza Strip and "voluntary migration" of the Palestinian population elsewhere. Ministers and parliamentarians discussed the re-establishment of Jewish settlements in Gaza and the north of the West Bank, known to some Israelis as Samaria. They were presented with maps and detailed preparations for the re-establishment of a Jewish presence in the areas inside what is considered internationally as the borders of a would-be Palestinian state.
