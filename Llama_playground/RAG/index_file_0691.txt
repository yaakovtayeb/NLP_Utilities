How many people are being held as hostages by Hamas, and who are they?

At 2023-oct-15, the Israeli military has confirmed 155 people are being held as hostages by Hamas. Among them are eight Germans, five US nationals, two Mexican nationals, and scores of Israelis. They include an 83-year-old Israeli journalist who was taken from his home in kibbutz Nir Oz with his 85-year-old partner, who had worked for decades for peace and the recognition of Palestinian rights.
