What is the recent acknowledgment made by Denmark's defence ministry regarding the airstrikes on Libya in 2011? 

Denmark's defence ministry acknowledged that its air force participated in airstrikes on Libya that killed 14 civilians in 2011, the first time any of the 10 countries involved in the Nato bombing campaign has acknowledged a possible link to non-combatant casualties.
