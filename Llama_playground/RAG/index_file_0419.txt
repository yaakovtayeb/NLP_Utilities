What happened in Gaza on December 15, 2023?

At 2023-dec-15, the Israel Defense Forces mistakenly identified three Israeli hostages as a threat and fired at them, killing them. The hostages were named as Yotam Haim, 28, Samer Fouad Al-Talalka, 25, and Alon Shamriz, 26. Additionally, Al Jazeera journalist Samer Abudaqa was killed in an Israeli missile attack on Farhana school in Khan Younis, southern Gaza, and two of the world’s largest shipping firms, Maersk and Hapag-Lloyd, suspended passage through a Red Sea strait vital for global commerce after Yemeni rebel attacks in the area. The US senator Bernie Sanders is demanding answers on Israel’s “indiscriminate” bombing across Gaza.
