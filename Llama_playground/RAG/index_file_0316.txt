What happened during David Lammy's speech at a Labour-linked thinktank's conference?

At 2024-jan-20, david Lammy's keynote speech was disrupted by protesters who held Palestinian flags as they shouted “ceasefire now” and criticized Labour’s stance on the Israel-Gaza war. Security officials took the protesters out of the building, but more protesters interrupted Lammy's speech. Lammy addressed their chants of “ceasefire now” head on and said that Labour has called for a sustainable ceasefire.
