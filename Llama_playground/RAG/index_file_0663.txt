What are some ways in which Arab and Jewish communities in Australia are responding to the conflict in Israel and Gaza?

At 2023-oct-21, arab and Jewish communities in Australia are responding to the conflict in Israel and Gaza by providing humanitarian aid, organizing and funding repatriation flights, making their voices heard and pressuring the Australian government to help citizens still in Israel and Palestine, and engaging in political action such as protests and marches. Some organizations like the Jewish National Fund of Australia and United Israel Appeal, as well as local synagogues, have also come together to fundraise for humanitarian aid.
