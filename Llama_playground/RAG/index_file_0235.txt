Who was Edward Said and why is he still inspiring people today?

At 2024-feb-15, edward Said was a Palestinian American literary scholar, political advocate, and concert-level classical pianist. He was a recognized literary scholar before the publication of Orientalism, the book that changed the landscape of cultural criticism, launched new fields of study, and challenged western representations of non-western peoples. He was also a fierce critic of the Palestinian Liberation Organization (PLO) chairman, Yasser Arafat. Today, people turn to Said for his ability to articulate a moral position with a sophisticated historical sensibility and to connect the Palestinian national movement to other anticolonial struggles across the world.
