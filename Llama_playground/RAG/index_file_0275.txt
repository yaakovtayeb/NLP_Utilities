What is the status of the reconstruction efforts in Antakya, Turkey, one year after the deadly earthquakes that hit southern Turkey and northern Syria?

At 2024-feb-04, the reconstruction efforts in Antakya, Turkey, one year after the deadly earthquakes that hit southern Turkey and northern Syria are slow and facing challenges. The government promised to build 319,000 new homes by February, but only 46,000 have been delivered so far. The rebuilding project is also criticized for its focus on exclusive new housing that locals fear will price them out.
