What happened during the overnight raid on al-Shifa hospital in Gaza City, and what were the claims made by the Israeli Defense Forces and Hamas?

At 2024-mar-18, israeli forces conducted a high-precision operation in limited areas of Shifa hospital based on intelligence information indicating the use of the hospital by senior Hamas terrorists to command attacks. The IDF claimed to have taken over the sprawling complex, killing or wounding an undisclosed number of Hamas militants and detaining 80 people. Hamas accused the Israeli military of directly targeting the hospital buildings without caring about patients, medical staff, or displaced people in it.
