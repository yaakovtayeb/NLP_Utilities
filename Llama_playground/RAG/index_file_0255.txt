Why did the head of the UN agency for Palestinian refugees fire nine staff members accused of being involved in Hamas's attacks without investigating the allegations first?

At 2024-feb-09, the head of the UN agency for Palestinian refugees, Philippe Lazzarini, fired nine staff members accused of being involved in Hamas's attacks without investigating the allegations first because he felt that the reputation and ability of the entire agency to continue to operate and deliver critical humanitarian assistance was at stake if he did not take such a decision.
