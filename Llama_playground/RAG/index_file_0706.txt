What is the current situation in Gaza amid the conflict between Hamas and Israel?

At 2023-oct-14, israel’s military delivered sweeping evacuation orders for almost half of Gaza’s 2.3 million people ahead of an expected ground offensive. Thousands of Palestinians began fleeing their homes and moving south. Israeli airstrikes on convoys fleeing Gaza City killed 70 people, mostly women and children. At least 1,900 Palestinians have been killed in Gaza from Israeli strikes, including 614 children and 370 women. Tens of thousands of protesters rallied across the Middle East and in parts of Asia, Europe and the United States on Friday in support of Palestinians and condemnation of Israel.
