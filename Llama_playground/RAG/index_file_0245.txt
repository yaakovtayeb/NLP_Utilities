What are the views of the majority of the public in Britain regarding the Israel-Hamas conflict? 

At 2024-feb-15, according to a More in Common poll conducted in late November and updated polling carried out in January, the majority of the public in Britain do not have a "side" in the Israel-Hamas conflict, with 16% sympathising more with the Israelis, 18% with the Palestinians, and the remaining two-thirds sympathising with neither side, or with both sides equally, or were unsure.
