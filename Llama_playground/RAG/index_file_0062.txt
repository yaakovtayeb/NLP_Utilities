What happened during the reported airstrikes in Iran and what was the response from various countries?

At 2024-apr-19, israel carried out airstrikes against Iran and explosions were reported in the sky over the cities of Isfahan and Tabriz. Calls mounted internationally for Israel and Iran to de-escalate their confrontation, and France, Italy, and Oman were quick to call for de-escalation. The US told the G7 foreign ministers meeting in Capri that it had received “last minute” information from Israel about a drone action in Iran.
