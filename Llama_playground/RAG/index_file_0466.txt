What is the current situation of the Israeli hostages being held in Gaza?

At 2023-dec-01, the families of the remaining 136 Israeli hostages being held in Gaza are worried about their safety after the end of a seven-day ceasefire, during which Hamas released 80 Israeli hostages in exchange for 240 Palestinian prisoners. The Israeli army confirmed the death of five hostages in Gaza and said that the remaining hostages include 17 women and children. The hostages are living in inhumane conditions and don't have access to their usual medications. The families of the hostages are grappling with feeling joy for those who have been released while being worried sick for loved ones left behind.
