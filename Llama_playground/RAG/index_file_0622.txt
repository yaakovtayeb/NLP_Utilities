What is the current situation of human rights abuses in Saudi Arabia and how is it related to the recent Tyson Fury and Francis Ngannou fight?

At 2023-oct-27, the article discusses the current situation of human rights abuses in Saudi Arabia, including the case of Loujain al-Hathloul, a female activist who was jailed for 1,001 days until her release in February 2021, and the perpetuation of the male guardianship system through the Personal Status Law. The article also mentions the recent Tyson Fury and Francis Ngannou fight, which is part of the country's efforts to showcase its modernization through sports, but the author argues that the fight and other sports events should not distract from the serious human rights issues in Saudi Arabia.
