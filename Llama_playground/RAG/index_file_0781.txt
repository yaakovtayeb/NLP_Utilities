What happened during Storm Daniel in Derna, Libya? 

At 2023-sep-13, storm Daniel hit the northern coast of Libya on Saturday night causing severe flooding in Derna and surrounding areas. The storm caused extensive damage to the road network, bridges, and buildings, and resulted in the deaths of thousands of people, with estimates ranging from 10,000 to 20,000. The storm also displaced tens of thousands of people, leaving them without food, water, and shelter. International aid agencies have been slow to respond to the disaster, and there are concerns about the lack of coordination between the rival governments in Libya.
