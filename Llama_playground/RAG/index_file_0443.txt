What is the main argument presented by Laurence McGowan in response to Howard Jacobson’s opinion piece on the use of the word genocide in relation to the conflict in the Middle East?

At 2023-dec-10, laurence McGowan finds Howard Jacobson’s suggestion to bar the use of the word genocide in relation to the actions of the state of Israel troubling. He wonders if the weight of the word should excuse people of Jewish descent from committing genocide themselves, and questions which alternative words might not cause such affront.
