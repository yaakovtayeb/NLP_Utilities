What happened to the Nepali students in the kibbutz in Alumim during the Hamas attack in Israel?

At 2023-oct-20, almost 50 Nepali students were staying in a kibbutz in Alumim when it was attacked by Hamas militants on 7 October. Ten of them died, mostly from gunshot wounds and grenade shrapnel, and their bodies were flown back to Kathmandu. One Nepali student is believed to have been taken hostage by Hamas.
