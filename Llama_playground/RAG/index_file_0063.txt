What was the recent military operation carried out by Israel against Iran and what was the response from Iran?

At 2024-apr-19, israel carried out a military operation against Iran and warned the Biden administration about the strike. Iran's air defence batteries were activated and there were reports of explosions near a major airbase close to the city of Isfahan. However, the Iranian government played down the scale of the attack and confirmed that there was no damage to any nuclear sites. Tensions between Iran and Israel remain high after Iran launched hundreds of drones and cruise missiles towards Israel in response to a strike on an Iranian diplomatic building in Damascus.
