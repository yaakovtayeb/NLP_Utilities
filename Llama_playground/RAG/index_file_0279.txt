What was the reason behind the joint airstrikes by the UK and US on Iran-linked Houthi targets in Yemen?

At 2024-feb-03, the joint airstrikes by the UK and US on Iran-linked Houthi targets in Yemen were designed to protect innocent lives and preserve freedom of navigation in the Red Sea amid Houthi attacks on boats. The Houthi group has repeatedly launched attacks on vessels in the Red Sea and elsewhere off the Yemen coast, endangering shipping on a key global trade route.
