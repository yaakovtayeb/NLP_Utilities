What is the situation of asylum seekers caught in the geopolitical drama between Russia and Finland?

At 2023-dec-11, over 900 people from conflict-ridden and impoverished countries, including Somalia, Syria, Yemen, and Iraq, have crossed the 830-mile (1,300km) frontier between Russia and Finland last month, and found themselves in the middle of an intensifying geopolitical drama. Helsinki closed the entire land border with its neighbor on 29 November for at least two weeks, accusing Moscow of using migrants in a “hybrid operation”.
