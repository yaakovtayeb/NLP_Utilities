What is Israel's current focus in Gaza and who are they targeting there?

Israel's current focus in Gaza is on the southern city of Khan Younis, which is being depicted as Hamas’s stronghold. They are targeting the Hamas leadership, including Yahya Sinwar and Mohammed Deif, who are believed to be hiding in the city.
