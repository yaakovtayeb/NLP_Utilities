What is the collaborative research related to drone technology that academics from the UK, Australia, and the US conducted with an Iranian university, and what are the implications of this research?

At 2024-feb-14, the collaborative research examined the use of drones in wireless networks and as communications hubs, which has direct military applications, according to security experts. The research was conducted with Sharif University of Technology in Tehran, which is under international financial sanctions and known for its close ties to the military. The funding agencies listed on the published study include government-backed research councils in the UK, EU, and Australia.
