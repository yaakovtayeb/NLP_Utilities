What happened at the Nova festival in Israel, and what is the current situation with the hostages?

At 2024-jan-06, the Nova festival in Israel was attacked by Hamas on October 7, resulting in the death of 364 people and the kidnapping of 40 others. The families of the hostages recently returned to the site to call for their return, and some hostages have been released in a ceasefire deal. However, negotiations broke down in December and the war between Israel and Hamas continues.
