What is the current state of Hamas after the Israeli offensive?
Hamas is significantly weaker than it was before the Israeli offensive. The group’s military infrastructure has been damaged, and a significant number of militants have been killed. However, they are still capable of firing rockets into southern Israel and many of the tunnels they have built under Gaza remain intact.
