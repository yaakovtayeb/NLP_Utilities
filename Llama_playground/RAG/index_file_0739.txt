What is the current state of relations between the US and Israel, and how has the recent Hamas attack affected them?

At 2023-oct-10, the US and Israel have recently strengthened their ties, with President Biden reaffirming their partnership in the wake of the Hamas attack on southern Israel. The US is poised to arm Israel, and the two leaders have committed to stay in regular contact. However, there are still complications, such as the issue of normalization of Israeli-Saudi relations and internal Democratic politics.
