What is the latest update on the Israel-Hamas conflict?

At 2023-oct-22, the latest update on the Israel-Hamas conflict includes the entry of a second aid convoy comprising of 14 trucks into Gaza, the urging of Pope Francis for the end of the war, the killing of three Palestinians in an Israeli air strike, and the accidental hitting of an Egyptian post by an Israeli tank. Additionally, thousands of people gathered in Berlin, London, and Paris to express their solidarity with Israel and Palestine, while the leaders of the US, UK, France, Germany, Italy, and Canada reiterated their support for Israel and its right to defend itself against terrorism.
