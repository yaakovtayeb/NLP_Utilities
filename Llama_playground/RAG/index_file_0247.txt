What is the significance of UN Security Council Resolution 242 in the context of the recognition of a Palestinian state? 

At 2024-feb-14, uN Security Council Resolution 242 serves as a bedrock for addressing the Palestinian question and maintaining the rejection of force as a way to gain territory. Its absence in the discourse of current Israeli designs on Gaza, the West Bank, and East Jerusalem suggests that the recognition of a Palestinian statelet would not be viewed as a fulfilment of international legal obligations by Israel, nor as a recognition of Palestinian aspirations for statehood.
