What is happening around the al-Shifa hospital in Gaza City?

At 2023-nov-11, israeli forces have been fighting Hamas gunmen around the al-Shifa hospital in Gaza City, which is considered a key strategic area. The Israeli government and military officials believe that seizing effective control of the hospital and the neighbourhood around it will prompt Hamas defences to collapse. There are reports of Israeli troops shooting at anyone outside or inside the hospital and preventing movement between buildings.
