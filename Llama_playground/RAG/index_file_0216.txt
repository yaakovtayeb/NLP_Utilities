What is the role of the Palestinian Scout Association in helping Gaza's population cope with life in makeshift shelters?

At 2024-feb-26, the Palestinian Scout Association is helping Gaza's population cope with life in makeshift shelters by teaching practical and teamwork skills, bulk-cooking and distributing food, making tents warmer and more waterproof, teaching basic first aid and safety measures, offering psychological support for traumatised children and adults, and reinstating basic language and maths lessons.
