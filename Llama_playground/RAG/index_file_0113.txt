Who was Maurice el Médioni and what was his contribution to Algerian music?

At 2024-apr-05, maurice el Médioni was a pianist who played a key role in the development of the Algerian styles chaabi and raï. He was a star in Algeria in the 1950s and provided a reminder that there was a time when Muslim and Jewish musicians worked together in Algeria to transform the country’s popular music. He eventually built an international reputation when in his 60s and played alongside some of his own musician friends and some British musicians in the resulting album, Café Oran (1996), which introduced his unique piano style to a new audience.
