Who is Esther Hayut and what is her role in Israel's supreme court?

At 2023-sep-24, esther Hayut is the chief justice of Israel's supreme court who is facing an existential crisis triggered by the Israeli government's judicial overhaul. She was born in 1953 in a ma'abara and became a judge in 1990, rising to the supreme court in 2004. She is sympathetic to class actions against large companies and discrimination cases, and strengthened protection laws for foreign workers.
