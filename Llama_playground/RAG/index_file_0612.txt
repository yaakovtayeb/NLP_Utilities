What is the current situation in Gaza amid the Israeli war?
People in Gaza are facing a life of isolation and desperation due to the Israeli war, with shortages of fuel and gas, limited access to electricity and water, and intense bombardment causing many to flee their homes and seek shelter wherever they can find it.
