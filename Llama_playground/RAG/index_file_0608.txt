What happened in Gaza after thousands of people raided UN warehouses in search of food? 

Order started to collapse in the besieged Gaza Strip after thousands of desperate people raided UN warehouses in search of food. Wheat, flour, and hygiene supplies were taken from four UN-run centers across the blockaded 25- by 7-mile strip, home to more than 2 million trapped people.
