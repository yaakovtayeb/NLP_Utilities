What is the current situation in Sudan and what action should be taken by the UN Security Council?

At 2023-sep-21, the conflict in Sudan has caused millions to be displaced and has resulted in a humanitarian crisis. The UN Security Council must intervene to stop the conflict and ensure access to critical humanitarian aid. The council should also hold those responsible for violations of human rights and humanitarian law accountable. The African Union should activate its early-warning mechanism to prevent further conflict.
