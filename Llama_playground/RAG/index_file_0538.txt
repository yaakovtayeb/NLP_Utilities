What is the current situation in Gaza and how many families have lost 10 or more members due to the bombardment?

At 2023-nov-12, according to the latest statistics provided by the Palestinian health ministry in Gaza, at least 312 families have lost 10 or more members due to the current bombardment of Gaza. At least 70% of Gaza's population has been displaced, and families have clustered together, crowding dozens of them into their homes in the hope they can avoid the heavy bombardment or at least pool increasingly scarce resources such as water, fuel and food.
