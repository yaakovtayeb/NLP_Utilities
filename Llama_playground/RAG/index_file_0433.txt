What is Hamas demanding in exchange for the release of hostages in Gaza and how long is Israel prepared to fight to defeat Hamas? 

At 2023-dec-10, hamas is demanding prisoner releases in exchange for the release of hostages in Gaza. Israel says there are still 137 hostages in Gaza, while activists say about 7,000 Palestinians are in Israeli jails. Israel has indicated that it is prepared to fight for months or longer to defeat Hamas in Gaza.
