What is the author's personal experience with the Israeli airstrikes in Gaza? 

At 2023-oct-24, the author experienced an Israeli airstrike in her mother-in-law's kitchen during her last visit to Gaza, and she also lived through two large-scale wars in Gaza in 2008-09 and 2012. Her mother died from a shortage of medical supplies and countless refused applications for Israeli military permits to travel out of Gaza for a scan.
