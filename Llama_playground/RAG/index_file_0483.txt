What did the London surgeon witness during his time in Gaza?

At 2023-nov-27, the London surgeon witnessed a "massacre unfold" during 43 days spent under bombardment in Gaza, saying the destruction of the Palestinian health system was a military objective of the war. He witnessed the use of white phosphorus munitions and claimed that the creation of an uninhabitable Gaza Strip was the aim and the destruction of all the components of modern life at which the health system lies was the main military objective.
