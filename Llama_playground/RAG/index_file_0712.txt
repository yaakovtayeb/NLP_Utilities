What happened during Israel's darkest day on October 13th?

At 2023-oct-13, hamas breached the Gaza-Israel barrier and launched a deadly attack on Israel, resulting in the deaths of 1,300 Israelis, including 247 soldiers, and over 1,500 Hamas fighters. The attack included the use of drones to disable communication towers and machine gun posts, creating openings for Hamas fighters to cross the barrier. The fighters then attacked military bases, kibbutzim, and a music festival, killing and taking hostages. The Israeli government responded with airstrikes and called reservists to arms, resulting in the destruction of many Hamas military operations.
