What is Egypt's latest effort to restart negotiations for a truce in the Gaza Strip and when did it happen?
Egypt was in contact with senior Hamas and Israeli figures as well as other mediators on Sunday, March 10, 2024, in an effort to restart negotiations for a truce in the Gaza Strip during Ramadan, which begins on Monday or Tuesday, two Egyptian security sources told Reuters.
