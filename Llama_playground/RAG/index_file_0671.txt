What happened at al-Ahli Arab hospital in Gaza and who were the victims of the blast?

At 2023-oct-18, a devastating blast occurred at al-Ahli Arab hospital in Gaza, which had been used as a refuge by thousands of people seeking shelter from the Israeli bombing campaign. The refugees, including many children, bore the brunt of the blast, with at least 471 people killed and more than 300 injured. The victims were torn apart or thrown across the hospital in the explosion.
