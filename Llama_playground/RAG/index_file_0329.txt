What is the Biden administration's stance on the Israel-Palestine conflict? 

At 2024-jan-19, the Biden administration aims to achieve a cessation of the war and establish a political pathway between Israel and Palestine. They also seek to provide urgent and large-scale emergency humanitarian aid into Gaza and encourage the transfer of tax revenue collected for the Palestinian Authority to the Authority. Additionally, the administration wants to reform the Palestinian Authority and rid it of corruption, promote internal elections, and revamp its institutions for better governance.
