What was Iran's response to the Israeli strike on Damascus that killed several Iranian officials?

At 2024-apr-02, iran's supreme national security council has decided on a "required" response to the Israeli strike on Damascus that killed three top commanders in Iran's al-Quds force and four other officers. The president, Ebrahim Raisi, who chaired the meeting, said Iran would retaliate at the time of its own choosing. The Iranian supreme leader, Ayatollah Ali Khamenei, also vowed revenge. However, no further details were provided about the meeting of the key decision-making body.
