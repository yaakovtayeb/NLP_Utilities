What was the reaction of the mourners at the funeral of Lili and Ram Itamari in Kfar Azza, and what did they say about the government's response to the Hamas attacks?

At 2023-oct-29, the mourners at the funeral of Lili and Ram Itamari in Kfar Azza were deeply grieved and vowed never to forgive or forget how families like the Itamaris were abandoned by the government. They accused the government of failing to come to the aid of people after the Hamas attacks and of focusing resources on the occupied West Bank in the months before.
