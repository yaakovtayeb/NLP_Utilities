What was the result of the vote on the Palestinian request for full UN membership, and why did the US veto it?

At 2024-apr-18, the vote in the 15-member security council was 12 in favor, the US opposed and two abstentions, the UK and Switzerland. The US vetoed the Palestinian request for full UN membership, stating that the emergence of a Palestinian state had to be the outcome of negotiations on all aspects of a Middle East peace settlement.
