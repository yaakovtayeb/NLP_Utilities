What happened in the drone attack on a US base in Jordan and what is the US response?

At 2024-jan-29, three US troops were killed and dozens more were wounded in a drone attack on a US base in Jordan. The US defense secretary Lloyd Austin has vowed to take “all necessary actions” to defend US troops. The Pentagon named the three troops killed in the attack as army reservists Sgt William Jerome Rivers, Spc Breonna Alexsondria Moffett and Spc Kennedy Sanders. The attack on Sunday was the first deadly strike against US troops since the Israel-Hamas war erupted in October, and marks a major escalation in tensions that have engulfed the Middle East.
