What happened during the Armistice Day protest in London and who were the groups involved?

At 2023-nov-11, the Armistice Day protest in London was marked by violence, with some far-right groups breaking violently through police lines and throwing missiles towards the Cenotaph. The Democratic Football Lads Alliance, a football hooligan network, and Tommy Robinson, founder of the anti-Muslim English Defence League, were among those present. The protest was also attended by pro-Palestinian demonstrators. The police arrested 82 people to prevent them from attacking the hundreds of thousands calling for a ceasefire in Gaza.
