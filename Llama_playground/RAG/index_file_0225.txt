What did the archbishop of Canterbury, Justin Welby, do when he found out that the Bethlehem-based Lutheran pastor Munther Isaac shared a platform with Jeremy Corbyn at a pro-Palestinian rally? 

At 2024-feb-21, the archbishop of Canterbury, Justin Welby, cancelled plans to meet the Bethlehem-based Lutheran pastor Munther Isaac, saying he could not meet him if he shared a platform with the former Labour leader Jeremy Corbyn at a pro-Palestinian rally, the pastor has said.
