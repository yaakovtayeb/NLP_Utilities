What is the current status of the ceasefire negotiations between Israel and Hamas in Gaza? 

At 2024-mar-16, hamas has abandoned key ceasefire demands and is now willing to accept a 40-day initial pause in hostilities. They are still demanding the release of between about 500 and 1,000 Palestinian prisoners from Israeli jails in return for 40 of more than 100 Israeli hostages that it is thought to be holding in Gaza. Meanwhile, Israeli officials have approved plans for a military assault on Rafah, Gaza’s southernmost city, which is Hamas’s last main stronghold but also now home to more than 1 million people.
