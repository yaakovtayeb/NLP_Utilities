What did Médecins Sans Frontières accuse the Libyan coastguard of doing during a rescue attempt in the Mediterranean?

At 2024-mar-18, médecins Sans Frontières accused the Libyan coastguard of hampering their attempt to save more than 170 people making the perilous journey across the sea to Europe by performing dangerous manoeuvres that put the people onboard, mostly Syrian refugees, at even greater risk.
