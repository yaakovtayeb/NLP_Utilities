What is the first international case related to the civil war in Syria that Canada and the Netherlands have brought before the ICJ?

At 2023-oct-10, the first international case related to the civil war in Syria that Canada and the Netherlands have brought before the ICJ is seeking urgent measures to stop the mistreatment of thousands of people still in detention and demanding that Syria stop all torture and arbitrary detention, open prisons to outside inspectors and provide information to families about the fate of their loved ones.
