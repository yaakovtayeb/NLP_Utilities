What happened to Niloofar Hamedi and Elaheh Mohammadi after they were released on bail in Iran? 

At 2024-jan-15, niloofar Hamedi and Elaheh Mohammadi, who were released on bail after 17 months in jail for reporting on the death of Mahsa Amini, have been charged under Iran's hijab laws for not wearing a hijab in pictures that were published of them celebrating their release with their heads uncovered.
