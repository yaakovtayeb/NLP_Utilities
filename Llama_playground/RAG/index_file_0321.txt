What is the US's stance on a ceasefire in Gaza, according to the White House? 

The US still opposes a ceasefire in Gaza, believing that such a move would help Hamas militants. The White House said that the US supports humanitarian pauses to get hostages out and more aid in, but they don’t support a ceasefire at this time.
