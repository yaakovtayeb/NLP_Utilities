What was the reaction of international leaders to the attack by Palestinian militants on Israel?

At 2023-oct-07, international leaders condemned the attack and called for restraint. The UN high commissioner for human rights called on Israel to avoid civilian casualties in its response. The US defence secretary stated that his organisation would send support to Israel. The French president offered his condolences to the victims, while his German counterpart Chancellor Olaf Scholz described the “terrifying news,” of the attack. The British foreign minister condemned the incursion. Egyptian officials said they were scrambling to contain the crisis diplomatically.
