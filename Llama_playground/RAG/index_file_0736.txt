What is the opinion of US politicians on the Hamas attack in Israel? 

At 2023-oct-10, uS politicians have varied opinions on the Hamas attack in Israel. Congresswoman Ilhan Omar condemned the attack but warned that the cycle of violence will continue without peace and justice in the Middle East. Congresswoman Cori Bush mourned the lives lost on both sides and called for an end to US government support for Israeli military occupation and apartheid. Meanwhile, a group of organizations at Harvard University blamed the Israeli government entirely for the unfolding violence, while Lawrence Summers, Harvard's president emeritus, criticized the university's leadership for not taking a stand in support of Israel.
