What is the significance of Prince William's statement regarding the war in Gaza?

At 2024-feb-21, prince William's statement regarding the war in Gaza is significant because it is an unprecedented intervention by a member of the royal family into the complex world of international diplomacy and the long, painful history on both sides of the Israeli-Palestinian conflict.
