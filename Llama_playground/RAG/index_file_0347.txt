What are the accusations made against Israel regarding the Gaza war and journalists?

At 2024-jan-11, israel's military is accused of targeting journalists in the Gaza war, resulting in the deaths of at least 79 journalists, mostly Palestinians and almost all at the hands of the Israel Defense Forces (IDF), according to the Committee to Protect Journalists. Leading press freedom groups and human rights organisations have called on Joe Biden to do more to pressure Israel to “abide by international law” and hold it accountable for the killings of reporters.
