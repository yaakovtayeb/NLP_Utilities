What is Grant Shapps' stance on the Red Sea being closed to commercial shipping and the possibility of further attacks by Houthi rebels in Yemen?

At 2024-jan-15, grant Shapps has stated that the UK will not accept the Red Sea being permanently closed to commercial shipping and that western forces might attack Houthi rebels in Yemen once more if further attacks occur. He also emphasized that the US-led airstrikes launched against the Houthis last week were designed to be a one-off military action designed to stop their attacks on commercial ships in the Red Sea.
