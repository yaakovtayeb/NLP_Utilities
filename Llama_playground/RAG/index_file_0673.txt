What is the UK government's stance on the blast at al-Ahli Arab hospital in Gaza?

At 2023-oct-18, the UK government is urging MPs not to rush to judgment over the blast at a hospital in Gaza, and British intelligence is working to analyze evidence and determine the cause of the explosion. The UK is also working closely with the government of Qatar to secure the release of hostages held by Hamas in Gaza. The UK government is urging people not to rush to judgment and is waiting for the definitive conclusions of the Foreign Office and MI6.
