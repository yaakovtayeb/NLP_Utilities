What is the reaction of the EU's climate tsar commissioner and Spain's environment minister to the latest draft text of Cop28 in Dubai?

At 2023-dec-11, they both find some elements in the text unacceptable and believe that deeper discussions with many other partners are needed. They also think that it is the beginning of a process that could last days and that the current text provides some reference to the science, but it is not consistent with dealing with energy.
