Can we trust the casualty figures from the Hamas-run Gaza health ministry?

At 2023-oct-26, there is a dispute over the reliability of the casualty figures reported by the Hamas-run Gaza health ministry, with some questioning their accuracy due to propaganda incentives and others defending their reliability based on past track records and independent investigations. The large proportion of women and children killed is indicative of high civilian casualties, but there is a grey area in differentiating combatants from civilians among the dead.
