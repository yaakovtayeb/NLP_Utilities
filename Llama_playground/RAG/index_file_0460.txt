What is the recent escalation of violence in the West Bank and how many Palestinians have been killed and injured by Israeli forces and settlers?

At 2023-dec-02, in recent weeks, there has been a marked escalation of violence by Israel in the West Bank, with Israeli forces and settlers killing 221 Palestinians and injuring another 2,955 people in the seven weeks from 7 October, according to the UN Office for the Coordination of Humanitarian Affairs. This is more than the entirety of 2022.
