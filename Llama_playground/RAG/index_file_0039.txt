What is the joint letter issued by 18 countries, including the US, regarding the Gaza crisis?

At 2024-apr-25, the joint letter issued by the leaders of 18 countries, including the US, calls for the immediate release of all hostages held by Hamas in Gaza for over 200 days. The letter also offers an immediate and prolonged ceasefire in Gaza in return for the release of the hostages. The letter emphasizes that the deal on the table to release the hostages would bring an immediate and prolonged ceasefire in Gaza and lead to the credible end of hostilities.
