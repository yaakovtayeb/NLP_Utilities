What is the current state of Europe's role in the Israeli-Palestinian conflict? 

At 2023-oct-30, the outbreak of the worst violence in the Israeli-Palestinian conflict for decades, triggered by Hamas’ attack on October 7 and Israel’s military response, marks a turning point in the unravelling of Europe’s role in the world. The tragic events in the region since October 7 have brutally exposed Europe’s contradictions. There has been a baffling cacophony of voices, from EU aid for the Palestinians being suspended and then restored, to ambiguous messaging on the need for Israel to defend itself within the limits of international humanitarian law.
