What is the possibility of a new ceasefire in Gaza and what are the terms of the draft agreement?

At 2024-feb-26, joe Biden believes a new, temporary ceasefire in Gaza is possible by next Monday. The draft agreement includes a 40-day pause in all military operations as well as the exchange of Palestinian prisoners for Israeli hostages at a ratio of 10 to one. It would also allow the gradual return of displaced civilians to northern Gaza, except men of military age.
