What is the US and Saudi Arabia's plan B for security and technology-sharing agreements, and what is the offer being made to Israel?

At 2024-may-01, the US and Saudi Arabia's plan B involves signing agreements on a bilateral defence pact, US help in the building of a Saudi civil nuclear energy industry, and high-level sharing in the field of artificial intelligence and other emerging technologies. An offer is being made to Israel of normalization of diplomatic relations with Riyadh in return for Israeli acceptance of the two-state solution to the 76-year Israeli-Arab conflict.
