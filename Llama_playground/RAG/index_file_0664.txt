What is the current situation in Gaza?

At 2023-oct-20, gaza is under a complete siege by Israel, with power, food, and water cut off to the 2.3 million inhabitants. The war has resulted in the death of more than 3,785 Palestinians and 12,500 wounded. The UN has called the blockade a war crime.
