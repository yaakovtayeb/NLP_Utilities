What happened in Derna, Libya on September 11th and what was the aftermath? 

At 2023-sep-21, on September 11th, two poorly maintained dams above the city of Derna, Libya burst due to torrential rains from Storm Daniel, resulting in devastating floods that killed an estimated 11,000 people and left thousands more missing. Many victims were buried in mass graves and their bodies remain unidentified. The aftermath includes ongoing rescue and relief efforts, as well as concerns about disease outbreaks.
