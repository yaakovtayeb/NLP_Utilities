What is the role of poetry in times of war according to Palestinian poet Najwan Darwish?
Poetry has been the colossal record of violence and … the colossal record of compassion. Darwish believes that writing is a testimony for history and reflects the reality of the colonizer and the people of the land. He also thinks that poetry is a way to confront history or face the injustices that are happening.
