What is the reason behind the blame Israel is facing from its regional allies over the Gaza hospital deaths?

At 2023-oct-18, israel is facing blame from its regional allies over the Gaza hospital deaths because Palestinian officials blamed Israel for the explosion, while Israel said it was caused by a failed rocket launch by the Palestinian Islamic Jihad militant group, which denied blame. Arab state foreign ministries have issued individual statements condemning Israel for the explosion, including the United Arab Emirates and Bahrain, which established ties with Israel in the Abraham Accords of 2020.
